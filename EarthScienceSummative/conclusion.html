Glacier Trends and Water Availability - Conclusion

Conclusion

This project shows glaciers' crucial role in water availability and ecosystem stability. Rapid glacier decline threatens freshwater supplies, agriculture, and biodiversity, causing water scarcity and affecting millions. Urgent action is needed, including further research, policy changes, sustainable water management, emission reductions, and international cooperation.
