Glacier Trends and Water Availability - Potential Solutions and Adaptations

Potential Solutions and Adaptations

To mitigate glacier melt impacts, we can improve water conservation, build reservoirs and ice stupas, and restore wetlands. Successful examples include ice stupas in the Himalayas and ancient irrigation in Peru. Governments should support sustainable policies and infrastructure, with community involvement crucial for implementation. Collaboration ensures future water availability.
