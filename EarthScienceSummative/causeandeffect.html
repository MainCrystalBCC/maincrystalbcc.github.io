Glacier Trends and Water Availability - Cause and Effect Relationships

Cause and Effect Relationships

Glacier melt is crucial for water availability, supporting rivers, lakes, and aquifers. As glaciers shrink due to global warming, meltwater decreases, reducing water supplies for irrigation, drinking, and hydropower in regions like the Himalayas and Andes. This impacts ecosystems, agriculture, and biodiversity, highlighting the interconnectedness of our climate systems.
