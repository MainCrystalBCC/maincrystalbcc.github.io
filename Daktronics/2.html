Instructions:

Show Control:

1. Turn on show Control by clicking "POWER" on the black box.

2. Then turn the screen on by clicking the button on the side.

3. Click on the file icon, for the actual system, its name is dmp-8video.

4. Wait for the 2 sets of text to disappear.

5. Click the Control Studio app and have fun!

Please note: these are test stock photos.

Livestream:

1. Turn on Livestream by clicking "POWER" on the gray box.

2. You can either use the controller on the bottom-left or use the boxes on the monitor to change what is visible.

3. Press "GO" to make it visible on livestream.

Please note: not all buttons work. These are test stock photos.

Scoreboard:

1. Click "START" to start the timer and "STOP" to stop the timer..

2. Click "SCORE +1" to add a point to that team.

3. Click "FOUL +1" to add a foul to that team.

4. Click "PERIOD +1" to add a period to the game.

3. Click "TIMER UP/DOWN" to change the direction of the timer.

Please note: not all buttons work.
